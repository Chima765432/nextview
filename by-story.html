Recommendations By Story

Films grouped by narrative structure, themes, and character development.

If you liked Parasite...

Try: Shoplifters

Both films explore family dynamics among people living on the margins of society. They share a narrative that shifts from warmth to unease, with stories about class, survival, and the bonds we form outside traditional family structures.

If you liked The Shawshank Redemption...

Try: The Count of Monte Cristo

Both feature protagonists who endure unjust imprisonment and find redemption through patience, intelligence, and resilience. The theme of hope against all odds runs through both narratives.

If you liked Inception...

Try: Primer

Both films feature layered, complex storytelling that rewards careful viewing. Primer is a low-budget film with one of the most intricate time-travel narratives ever written. Like Inception, it trusts the audience to keep up.
