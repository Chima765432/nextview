How NextView Works

Most recommendation sites group movies by genre, popularity, actors, or ratings without understanding what actually made you connect with a film. NextView is different.

We ask the real question: why did you love that movie? Was it the plot structure, the character arcs, the themes, or the atmosphere? Based on your answer, we suggest films that share those same qualities.

This site includes international films, anime, and media from around the world as part of its core content.
